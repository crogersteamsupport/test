CDI Rating

The CDI (or Customer Distress Index) is a proactive tool you can use to quickly determine the overall health of your customers. This is where you will weigh the metrics, and the results can be viewed on each customer record. Detailed setup instructions can be found here. (http://www.teamsupport.com/documentation/1/en/topic/customer-distress-index-cdi)

The CDI runs once per day, and the last time your account processed was: NEVER. To force an update now, please click the force update button below.

Rating

The Rating system allows your team to get feedback from your customers about their satisfaction with how a ticket was handled. You will be able to see the results for each of your internal staff of users as well as on a customer basis.
